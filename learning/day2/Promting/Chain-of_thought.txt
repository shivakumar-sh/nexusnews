A store gives a 20% discount on a $50 item. After that, 10% tax is added.
What is the final price?

Think step by step before answering.



Start with the original price: **$50**

1. Apply the 20% discount:

   * 20% of 50 = 0.20 × 50 = 10
   * Discounted price = 50 − 10 = **$40**

2. Add 10% tax to the discounted price:

   * 10% of 40 = 0.10 × 40 = 4
   * Final price = 40 + 4 = **$44**

So, the final price is **$44**.
