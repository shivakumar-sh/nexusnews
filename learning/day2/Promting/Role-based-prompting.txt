# Social Media Campaign: Launching a Fitness App for College Students

## Campaign Overview

**Campaign Name:** *Fit Between Classes*
**Target Audience:** College students ages 18–24
**Primary Goal:** App downloads and active weekly users
**Secondary Goals:** Brand awareness, campus engagement, referral growth

### Brand Positioning

A fitness app designed for busy student life:

* Fast workouts
* Affordable/free
* Beginner-friendly
* Social accountability
* Stress relief and wellness support

### Core Message

> “You don’t need hours at the gym. You need a routine that fits your schedule.”

---

# Campaign Objectives

## 1. Awareness

Introduce the app as the “college lifestyle fitness app.”

**KPIs**

* Reach
* Impressions
* Video views
* Follower growth

## 2. Acquisition

Drive installs through relatable student-focused content.

**KPIs**

* App downloads
* Cost per install (CPI)
* Click-through rate

## 3. Engagement & Retention

Build habits through community participation.

**KPIs**

* Daily active users
* Challenge participation
* UGC submissions
* Referral shares

---

# Target Audience Segments

### Freshmen

* Looking for routines and social belonging
* Interested in confidence and wellness

### Busy Students

* Need quick workouts between classes
* Prefer convenience and flexibility

### Social Fitness Users

* Motivated by streaks, friends, and challenges

### Beginners

* Intimidated by gym culture
* Want easy entry points

# Platform Strategy

## TikTok

**Primary channel**

### Content Types

* “Workout between classes” videos
* Dorm-room fitness routines
* Meme-based fitness humor
* Trending audio challenges
* Before/after productivity stories

### Posting Frequency

2–3 posts daily

### Example Hooks

* “POV: You only have 12 minutes before biology”
* “The campus gym is packed, so here’s Plan B”
* “Trying to survive finals week without losing my mind”

---

## Instagram

**Lifestyle + community building**

### Content Types

* Reels
* Student transformations
* Carousel tips
* Motivational stories
* Polls and quizzes

### Posting Frequency

1–2 feed posts daily + Stories throughout day

### Story Ideas

* “Pick tomorrow’s workout”
* “Rate your energy level this week”
* Countdown to campus challenge launches

---

## YouTube

**Long-form trust building**

### Content Types

* 10-minute beginner workouts
* “Healthy college day in my life”
* Stress management content
* Fitness myth-busting

### Posting Frequency

2 videos weekly

---

## Snapchat

**Daily engagement**

### Content Types

* Campus geo-filters

* Exclusive promo codes
